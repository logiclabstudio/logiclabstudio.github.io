Our Story

Cooked with
soul since day one.

Sima's Kitchen started as a trailer corner spot with one goal: feeding the kasi real food, made fresh, made with love. No shortcuts, no compromises. From early morning magwinyas to afternoon plates of stew — every dish carries that home kitchen feeling.

Located in the heart of the community, we've become more than a food stop — we're a gathering place. Pull up, eat well, leave happy.
