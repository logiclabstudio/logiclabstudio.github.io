06 FAQ

Questions
answered.

How long does a website take? +
Starter pages can be 2–5 days if content is ready. Business sites are usually 1–2 weeks. Product/MVP depends on scope, but we ship in weekly sprints with demos so you see progress throughout.
What do you need from me to start? +
Business name, services, contact details, logo (if you have one), and examples of sites you like. If you don't have content ready, we can help structure it for you.
Do you do SEO? +
Yes — we handle the technical basics: metadata, clean structure, speed, and mobile optimization. Full SEO strategy and content is a bigger engagement, but we can quote that separately.
Can you connect payments, booking, or WhatsApp? +
Yes. WhatsApp CTAs are quick to set up. Bookings and payments depend on the provider you choose — PayFast, Peach Payments, Yoco links, etc. We integrate and test them all.
Who owns the website and domain? +
You own your domain and all content. We deliver your site files and can host/maintain it for you, or hand everything over if you'd rather manage it yourself.
