Our Mission

To love God and love people

We exist to help people encounter Jesus Christ, grow in their faith, and serve their community with compassion and excellence.

"Love the Lord your God with all your heart and with all your soul and with all your mind. This is the first and greatest commandment. And the second is like it: Love your neighbor as yourself." Matthew 22:37-39
Our Vision

A community transformed by grace

To be a welcoming church where everyone can belong, grow spiritually, and make a difference in the world around them.

What We Believe

Our foundation in biblical truth and Christian doctrine.

The Bible

God's Inspired Word

The Bible is God's inspired Word, authoritative for faith and practice, revealing His plan for salvation through Jesus Christ.

Jesus Christ

Our Savior & Lord

Jesus is fully God and fully man, born of a virgin, lived a sinless life, died for our sins, rose again, and will return in glory.

Salvation

By Grace Through Faith

Salvation is a free gift of God's grace, received through faith in Jesus Christ, not by works or human merit.
